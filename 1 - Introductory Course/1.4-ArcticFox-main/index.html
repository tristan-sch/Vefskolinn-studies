Arctic Skiing

Under the light of the Midnight Sun or under the glowing Northern Lights in Winter, you will experience skiing like never before. Surrounded by stunning mountains and the Arctic Ocean, it is a wild adventure.

Ice Caving

Join this expedition to explore an Ice Cave under a Volcano. You are joining an adventure with endless possibilities. The view inside the Ice Cave will amaze you with the combination of the colors of the ice.

Aurora Hunting

This fascinating natural phenomenon known as the Northern Lights, is one of the most mesmerizing sights you will ever come across in your lifetime. The dancing colors and shapes in the dark arctic sky will simply leave you speechless.

Glacier Snow - mobiling

Experience Iceland from above. Push deep into icy wilderness and glide across the glacier snow. You will find yourself in a land surrounded by myths, with exceptional views, rock formations and majestic ice sculptures.

Super Jeep Adventure

They are called Super Jeep for their modifications allow them to drive to impossible places. The can drive through ice, deep snow, mud, or volcanic sand. They can cross the arctic torrents and pass through harsh lava fields to get into the highlands. A true adventure.

Highland Trekking

Trek in some of the most stunning scenery in the world in remote and wild Icelandic nature. Go off the beaten path and enjoy pure solitude and ocean views. Hike through the wilderness and discover incredible landscapes.

Active Volcano

The experience of a lifetime, witness an active volcanic eruption and molten lava, spewing and flowing at Geildingadalir Valley. Iceland, located in between the mid-Atlantic ridge is truly the land of Fire and Ice.

Whale Watching

Discover the magnificent nature of Iceland where you can see whales, dolphins and sea birds in their natural habitat. On a kayak or in a traditional oak boat, explore the home of the whales in a natural scenery.
